# Rielcode

**Websites with uncommon polish.**

Rielcode builds custom websites, landing pages, and simple e-commerce, designed and developed end-to-end.

## What we do

Rielcode is a one-person web studio offering editorial-grade websites, landing pages, and simple e-commerce. Every project is designed and built by the same person, end-to-end — no handoffs, no agency layers. Pricing is published; engagements are productized so clients know cost and timeline before they enquire.

Service categories:

- Single-page landing pages engineered to convert
- Bespoke multi-page custom websites
- Simple e-commerce storefronts with payment and inventory
- Add-ons (revisions, blog setup, multilingual, audits, hosting, monthly retainer)

## Services and pricing

### Landing — IDR 4,000,000 / $260 — 1–2 weeks

A single page, engineered to convert. Copy, design, build, ship.

Deliverables:

- Single high-conversion page
- Mobile-first design
- Copy consultation
- Contact form or CTA integration
- 2 rounds of revisions
- 14-day post-launch support

### Custom — IDR 8–12M / $520–780 — 3–4 weeks

A bespoke multi-page website, designed and developed end-to-end.

Deliverables:

- Up to 6 pages
- Bespoke design end-to-end
- Static site (Astro) or custom CMS
- SEO foundations
- 2 rounds of revisions per page
- 14-day post-launch support

### E-commerce — IDR 15M+ / $980+ — 4–6 weeks

Simple storefronts for small catalogues. Payment + inventory included.

Deliverables:

- Product catalogue (up to 30 SKUs)
- Payment gateway integration
- Inventory management basics
- Order confirmation emails
- 2 rounds of revisions
- 14-day post-launch support

## Studio

Rielcode is run by one designer-developer. The studio page covers the way we work, the people behind it, and frequently asked questions. Process is intentionally compact: brief → design → build → ship → 14-day post-launch support.

## Work

Case studies live at `/work` with individual pages at `/work/{slug}`. Each case study covers the brief, the approach, and the outcome.

## Contact and engagement

Three entry points depending on intent:

- `/contact` — general enquiry form (throttled, no payment)
- `/order` — start a productized package (Landing / Custom / E-commerce) with deposit and confirmation flow
- `/custom-plan` — bespoke enquiry for scope that doesn't fit a productized package

After an order, clients receive token-gated portals for brief intake (`/brief`), progress tracking (`/progress`), and a testimonial request (`/testimonial`) once the project is delivered. Invoices are publicly viewable at `/i/{invoiceNumber}` with a downloadable PDF.
